Where can I play pickleball in Lisbon?

In Lisbon, you can play pickleball at Lisboa Racket Centre (2 courts) and Oeiras Padel Academy (1 court). Both facilities offer professional equipment and extended hours.

What is the cost to play pickleball in Portugal?

Prices vary between facilities. For example, Lagos Tenis Center charges €5 per session. Many facilities offer free lessons for beginners, such as in Viana do Castelo.

What are the largest pickleball centers in Portugal?

The largest center is Rocha Brava Racquet Club in the Algarve with 12 courts. Other major facilities include NAVE | Padel x Pickleball in Lisbon, Clube Pickleball Leiria in Leiria, and Lagos Tenis Center in the Algarve, each with 8 courts.

Is pickleball available 24 hours in Portugal?

Yes, some facilities operate 24 hours. Lagoa Pickleball in Faro offers 24-hour access to their pickleball courts.
